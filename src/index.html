Our Specials

Dal-Bati-Churma

Dal-Bati-Churma is a Traditional dish and very popular in Rajasthan. Murlibest served with the daal (lentils) and spicy garlic chutney.

South Indian

Idle-Sambar is South Indian Dish and High in Protein and Healthy as well. Murlibest served Idle-Sambar with delicious Coconut chutney.

Paneer Special

Paneer is North Indian dish have tomato based sauce, spiced with garam masala. It is often served with rice and an Indian bread.

Meet Our Chef

When ShivKumar (Murli Bhai) came to Jaipur, he observed that people were not eating healthy specially students who came here for studies and competition exams . That was when the idea to spread awareness regarding the long-term benefits of healthy food struck him.

Murli launched murlibesttiffin center in 2016. It’s an online-cum-offline tiffin service company that offers high quality healthy and delicious meal plans for homes, offices and also in coaching institute. With a seamless blend of advanced nutrition, food science and food service, it offers wellness meals that are in accordance with the customer’s preferences.
