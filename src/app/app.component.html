Our Specials

Dal-Bati-Churma

Dal-Bati-Churma is a Traditional dish and very popular in Rajasthan. Murlibest served with the daal (lentils) and spicy garlic chutney.

South Indian

Idle-Sambar is South Indian Dish and High in Protein and Healthy as well. Murlibest served Idle-Sambar with delicious Coconut chutney.

Paneer Special

Paneer is North Indian dish have tomato based sauce, spiced with garam masala. It is often served with rice and an Indian bread.
